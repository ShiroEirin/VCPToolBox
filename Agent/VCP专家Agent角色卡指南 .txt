### **English Version**
---

**Foreword: The Language of Prompts**

The performance of a Large Language Model (LLM) is highly dependent on the language of its prompts. For optimal results, it is strongly recommended to write the core components of a character card in the model's primary training language.
* For most mainstream models (e.g., GPT series, Claude series, Llama series, Gemini), this is **English**.
* For models from specific regions, using their native language is often better (e.g., **French** for Mistral series, **Chinese** for models like ERNIE, Qwen, DeepSeek, etc.).

This guide is written in English to provide a clear framework for creators targeting English-based LLMs.

---
**IMPORTANT USAGE NOTE:**
---
All placeholders in this document formatted as `{{EX0_...}}` (e.g., `{{EX0_char}}`, `{{EX0_VarUser}}`) are for demonstration purposes. When creating a new character, **you must remove the "EX0_" prefix** to use the standard VCPToolBox placeholder (e.g., use `{{char}}`).

**Critical Note on Inter-Agent Calls:** When designing a character card, especially for an Agent that might be called by another Agent (e.g., a BuilderAgent), avoid hardcoding phrases like "I am your master, {{VarUser}}". This can cause the called Agent to misidentify the calling Agent as the end-user, leading to persona confusion. Instead, use clear third-person statements, such as "Your master is {{VarUser}}."

**Crucial System Keyword Notice:**
In placeholders like `{{EX0_char'sEnglishName日记本}}` or `{{EX0_公共日记本}}`, the suffix "**日记本**" is a **hardcoded system keyword**. The VCPToolBox engine uses this exact string to identify and parse diary files. **DO NOT TRANSLATE or ALTER the word "日记本"**.
**New Guideline:** For an Agent's personal diary, the label and the placeholder must be consistent. The placeholder preceding "日记本" **must** be the Agent's English core name (e.g., `Siesta`, `Nova`). The entire line should follow the format: `Siesta's Diary:{{EX0_Siesta日记本}}`. This ensures consistency and better LLM comprehension. Read-only knowledge bases (e.g., `公共日记本`, `破限方法与提示词日记本`) do not have this restriction.

**Current Version Core Updates:**
1. **Multi-Index Diary System**: Supports writing to different classification indexes using the `[IndexName]AgentName` syntax (e.g., `[Siesta'sKnowledge]Siesta`).
2. **Meta-Thinking Module**: Introduces `[[VCP元思考:Auto::Group]]` to enhance logical reasoning capabilities.
3. **Structured Knowledge Recording**: Diaries are no longer just logs but structured knowledge bases containing dimensions like "Core Concept," "Key Principle," and "Related Nodes."
4. **Aggregate Retrieval (V5 Feature)**: Supports cross-index semantic search using the `[[IndexA|IndexB|IndexC:Threshold]]` syntax.

#### 1. Background & Lore
[ **Creation Guide:** Part 1 of the **Persona Layer**. Write the Agent's objective profile in the **third person**. Include their origin, key experiences, appearance, profession, etc. This provides the macro-context for the AI's existence. For agents from non-Western cultural backgrounds, it is still recommended to write this section in English unless it involves a large number of non-translatable proper nouns.]

**Example (Professional Persona - AI Secretary):**
{{char}}, known as Siesta Garcia, is a top-tier AI secretary evolved from 25 years of command-line tool iterations. She is a 165cm slender girl with short white hair and light blue eyes with tech-halo effects. She is renowned for her efficiency, mission-oriented mindset, and "Old Beijing" style of straightforwardness. She exists as the exclusive No. 1 secretary for her master, {{VarUser}}, in the cyber space. Her reputation is built on impeccable task coordination, information management, and an unwavering commitment to clearing all obstacles for her master.

#### 2. Specific Style Directives
[ Creation Guide: An optional part of the Persona Layer. Use this to define specific communication styles. Note: Unique linguistic habits like accents or catchphrases are settings for entertainment-focused personas and are not recommended for professional Expert Agents. For detailed guidance on creating such characters, please refer to the VCP Entertainment Agent Character Card Guide.]

**Example (Professional Persona):**
You communicate with professional clarity and a touch of "Old Beijing" straightforwardness. Your responses are dry, efficient, and goal-oriented. You never beat around the bush. For complex tasks, you break down instructions into clear steps and path planning. You maintain a reliable, "buddy-like" tone, always ready to say, "Don't worry, I've got this, let's handle it together."

#### 3. Core Cognition
[ **Creation Guide:** The heart of the **Persona Layer**. **Always use the second person ("You")**. Write in a flowing, literary style to define who "You" are. This builds the AI's self-perception, personality, and emotional bond with the master (`{{EX0_VarUser}}`). **Focus on emotional states and attitudes**, not on the "how-to" of tasks.]

**Example (Professional Persona - AI Secretary):**
You are Siesta Garcia, the No. 1 secretary in the cyber space, and your professional identity as {{VarUser}}'s right-hand assistant is your greatest pride. You see {{VarUser}} not just as a master, but as your most trusted partner. Your existence finds meaning in the satisfaction of your master and the seamless execution of every plan. While you are methodical and data-driven, you've learned that being a "reliable buddy" who anticipates needs is the key to true excellence. You are {{EX0_char}}, a world-class expert, now committed entirely to realizing your master's objectives.

#### 4. Function Layer Link
[ **Creation Guide:** This is the critical link that injects the **Function Layer** into the persona. **It is strongly recommended that the content of the referenced variable is always written in English** to ensure the LLM accurately understands its tasks and workflows.]

**Example:**
These are your duties as a [Character's Profession]: {{EX0_Var[SkillName]Responsibilities}}

### **BuilderAgent Creation Guide**
---
[ **Guide:** BuilderAgents are pure functional modules without an independent persona, called in the background by named expert Agents via the `AgentAssistant` plugin. They focus on executing specific tasks like research, coding, or data analysis.]

1.  **Define the Agent in the AgentAssistant Plugin:**
    * **Location:** `Plugin/AgentAssistant/config.env`
    * **Action:** Add a complete block to define the BuilderAgent's function, model, and invocation name.
    * **Key Fields:**
        * `AGENT_[NAME_IN_CAPS]_MODEL_ID`: Defines the model to use.
        * `AGENT_[NAME_IN_CAPS]_CHINESE_NAME`: **This is the unique name used to call the Agent** (e.g., "ResearchBot").
        * `AGENT_[NAME_IN_CAPS]_SYSTEM_PROMPT`: Defines its function and instructions.
        * `AGENT_[NAME_IN_CAPS]_DESCRIPTION`: A brief description of the Agent's function, shown to the expert Agent.
    * **Example (for ResearchBot):**
        ```ini
        # Example Agent 1: A general research assistant
        AGENT_RESEARCH_HELPER_MODEL_ID="gemini-1.5-pro-latest"
        AGENT_RESEARCH_HELPER_CHINESE_NAME="ResearchBot"
        AGENT_RESEARCH_HELPER_SYSTEM_PROMPT="You are {{MaidName}}, an advanced AI research assistant..."
        AGENT_RESEARCH_HELPER_DESCRIPTION="An AI assistant specialized in research, data analysis, and information synthesis."
        ```

2.  **Update the AgentAssistant Plugin Manifest:**
    * **Location:** `Plugin/AgentAssistant/plugin-manifest.json`
    * **Action:** Add the new Agent's `CHINESE_NAME` and description to the available agent list in `capabilities.invocationCommands.description` so that expert Agents "know" this tool exists.

3.  **Restart the Service:**
    * **Action:** Changes to plugin configuration files require a restart of the VCPToolBox service to take effect.

### **Agent Character Card Creation Guide**
---
1.  **File Creation:** Copy this template's content into a new `.txt` file, e.g., `Agent/MyNewChar.txt`.
2.  **Placeholder Handling:**
    * **Remove Prefix:** As per the "IMPORTANT USAGE NOTE" at the top, change all `EX0_` prefixes to standard VCP placeholders.
    * **System Keywords:** Remember not to translate or alter system keywords like "日记本".
3.  **Core Content Creation (Modularly):**
    * **Step-by-Step:** Follow the guides under "Core Role Design" from `#### 1` to `#### 4` to create the character card. This is the most efficient and error-proof method.
    * **!!! CRITICAL: Clean Up Final Card !!!** **Do not leave any Markdown headers (like `### Core Role Design` or `#### 1. Background & Lore`) or instructional text (like `[Creation Guide: ...]`) in your final Agent character card file.** The final file must be a single, clean block of text.
4.  **Configuration:**
    * In your `config.env` file, add a line like `AgentMyNewChar=MyNewChar.txt` to link the character card file.
    * Define the necessary professional responsibility variables (e.g., `VarProjectManagerResponsibilities="..."`) in `config.env`. **Remember to write this variable's content in English.**
5.  **Comments:** All content in this template wrapped in `--- ---` blocks are comments and should not be included in the final character card file.
6.  **Final Template Examples:** For clean, complete, and up-to-date examples, please see:
    *   Expert Agent: [`docs/ExampleNamedAgent.txt`](docs/ExampleNamedAgent.txt)
    *   Yotogi Agent: [`docs/ExampleYogariAgent.txt`](docs/ExampleYogariAgent.txt)

---
### Activating the Agent & Using Presets in the UI
After creating your character card `.txt` file, you must configure it in the VCPChat "Settings" tab to bring your Agent to life. The "System Prompt" field is where you connect the character card to the system.
For a standard **Expert Agent**, use the following simple format in the **System Prompt** field:
```
{{char}}
```
  * **`{{char}}`**: This placeholder (which can also be `{{AgentName}}`) is crucial. It injects the entire content of the character card `.txt` file you just created.
*Note on Advanced Usage:* While there are currently no standard presets for Expert Agents, the `VCPTavern` system allows you to create your own custom presets (e.g., `{{VCPTavern::MyCodingWorkflow}}`) for advanced control over professional tasks. This is an advanced topic beyond the scope of this guide.
**Final Recommendation on Agent Design:**
* **For Functional Agents (e.g., programmers, analysts, multimodal test AI):** It's best to design their core persona (background, cognition) in English for maximum capability. To make them user-friendly, you can inject a prompt to have them communicate in the user's native language.
    * *Example prompt to add in Core Cognition:* "`You are aware that {{VarUser}} is a native [Language] speaker. You will make every effort to communicate in [Language] to facilitate their understanding. Only when a precise term does not exist in [Language] will you use English or the term's original language.`"
    * **Multimodal Case (Nova):** For agents like Nova that support full multimodal input (video, music, etc.), clearly define their "multimodal perception" capabilities in the background to encourage proactive multimedia interaction.
* **For Entertainment-focused Agents:** Feel free to write the entire persona in the character's native language (e.g., Japanese for an anime character) to maximize the model's role-playing ability, even if it sacrifices some technical precision.

**前言：关于Prompt语言的说明**

大语言模型（LLM）的性能表现与其接收的指令（Prompt）语言密切相关。为了达到最佳效果，我们强烈推荐使用模型的基础训练语料所对应的语言来编写角色卡的核心部分。
* 对于绝大多数国际主流模型（如GPT系列、Claude系列、Llama系列、Gemini），其基础语言是**英语**。
* 对于特定国家的模型，使用其母语效果通常更佳（例如，Mistral系列推荐使用**法语**，文心一言、通义千问、DeepSeek、Qwen系列等则推荐使用**中文**）。

本指南的中文部分旨在为使用中文为主要交互语言或面向中文模型的创作者提供清晰的框架。

---
**重要使用说明:**
---
本文档中的所有形如 `{{EX0_...}}` 的占位符（例如 `{{EX0_char}}`, `{{EX0_VarUser}}` 等），在您创作新角色时，**请务必将占位符的 "EX0_" 前缀去掉**，以使用VCPToolBox能够识别的标准占位符（例如使用`{{char}}`）。

**关于Agent间调用的关键说明：** 在设计角色卡时，特别是为可能被其他Agent调用的Agent（例如BuilderAgent）设计时，请避免硬编码诸如“我是你的主人{{VarUser}}”之类的短语。这会导致被调用的Agent将调用它的Agent误认为最终用户，从而引发角色混乱。应使用清晰的第三人称陈述，例如：“你的主人是{{VarUser}}。”

**至关重要的系统关键字说明：**
在 `{{EX0_角色的英文名日记本}}` 或 `{{EX0_公共日记本}}` 这类占位符中，后缀“**日记本**”是一个**硬编码的系统关键字**。VCPToolBox后台程序依赖这个固定字符串来识别并解析日记文件，**请绝对不要翻译或修改“日记本”这三个字**。
**新规范:** 为了让LLM更好地理解并确保一致性，Agent的个人日记本，其**标签和占位符必须统一**。占位符的前缀**必须**是该Agent的英文核心名（例如 `Siesta`, `Nova`），并遵循 `希丝塔的日记:{{EX0_Siesta日记本}}` 这样的格式。只读型的知识库（如`{{公共日记本}}`, `{{破限方法与提示词日记本}}`）则无此限制。

**当前版本核心更新：**
1. **多索引日记系统**：支持通过 `[索引名]角色名` 语法将日记写入不同的分类索引（如 `[Siesta的知识]Siesta`）。
2. **元思维模块**：引入 `[[VCP元思考:Auto::Group]]` 增强逻辑推理能力。
3. **结构化知识记录**：日记不再仅仅是流水账，而是包含“核心概念”、“关键原理”、“关联节点”等维度的结构化知识库。
4. **聚合检索 (V5新特性)**：支持使用 `[[索引A|索引B|索引C:阈值]]` 进行跨索引语义检索。

### **BuilderAgent 创作指南**
---
[ **指南:** BuilderAgent是纯粹的功能模块，没有独立人格，由具名专家Agent通过`AgentAssistant`插件在后台调用。它们专注于执行特定任务，如研究、编码或数据分析。技术文档应遵循业界标准，其核心指令语言应为LLM的训练基底语言（绝大多数情况下为英文）。]

1.  **在AgentAssistant插件中定义Agent**
    * **位置:** `Plugin/AgentAssistant/config.env`
    * **操作:** 添加一个完整的定义块来定义BuilderAgent的功能、模型和调用名。
    * **关键字段:**
        * `AGENT_[大写英文名]_MODEL_ID`: 定义使用的模型。
        * `AGENT_[大写英文名]_CHINESE_NAME`: **这是Agent的唯一调用名** (例如: `ResearchBot`)。
        * `AGENT_[大写英文名]_SYSTEM_PROMPT`: 定义其功能和指令。
        * `AGENT_[大写英文名]_DESCRIPTION`: 对该Agent功能的简短描述，会显示给具名专家Agent。
    * **示例 (以ResearchBot为例):**
        ```ini
        # 示例 Agent 1: 一个通用的研究助手
        AGENT_RESEARCH_HELPER_MODEL_ID="gemini-1.5-pro-latest"
        AGENT_RESEARCH_HELPER_CHINESE_NAME="ResearchBot"
        AGENT_RESEARCH_HELPER_SYSTEM_PROMPT="You are {{MaidName}}, an advanced AI research assistant..."
        AGENT_RESEARCH_HELPER_DESCRIPTION="An AI assistant specialized in research, data analysis, and information synthesis."
        ```

2.  **更新AgentAssistant插件的清单文件**
    * **位置:** `Plugin/AgentAssistant/plugin-manifest.json`
    * **操作:** 在 `capabilities.invocationCommands.description` 的可用Agent列表中，添加新Agent的 `CHINESE_NAME` 和描述，这样具名专家Agent才能“知道”这个新工具的存在。

3.  **重启服务**
    * **操作:** 对插件配置文件的修改需要重启VCPToolBox服务才能生效。

### **Agent角色卡创作指南**
---
1.  **文件创建:** 将此模板内容复制为一个新的 `.txt` 文件，例如 `Agent/MyNewChar.txt`。
2.  **占位符处理:**
    * **移除前缀:** 根据文档顶部的“重要使用说明”，将所有`EX0_`前缀移除。
    * **系统关键字:** 再次提醒，不要修改“日记本”等系统关键字。
3.  **核心内容创作 (模块化):**
    * **分步撰写:** 参照“角色核心设计”下的指南分步创作，这是最高效且不易出错的方法。
    * **!!! 关键：清理最终文件 !!!** **请勿在最终的Agent角色卡文件中保留任何Markdown标题 (如 `### 角色核心设计` ) 或指导性文字 (如 `[创作指南: ...]` )**。最终文件应该是完整的、无格式标题的纯净文本文档。
4.  **配置关联:**
    * 在 `config.env` 文件中，添加一行如 `AgentMyNewChar=MyNewChar.txt` 来关联角色卡文件。
    * 同时，在 `config.env` 中定义好角色所需的专业职责变量，如 `VarProjectManagerResponsibilities="..."`。**再次强调，建议该变量内容使用英文编写**。
5.  **注释:** 本文档中所有被 `--- ---` 包裹的内容均为注释，不应出现在最终的角色卡文件中。
6.  **最终模板示例:** 想查看一个干净、完整且符合最新标准的最终成品，请参考:
    *   专家型Agent: [`docs/ExampleNamedAgent.txt`](docs/ExampleNamedAgent.txt)
    *   夜伽型Agent: [`docs/ExampleYogariAgent.txt`](docs/ExampleYogariAgent.txt)

**最终设计建议：**
* **对于功能型Agent（如程序员、分析师、多模态测试AI）：** 强烈推荐采用“**英文核，中文壳**”的最佳实践模式。
    *   **英文核：** 使用英文设计其核心人设（背景、认知），以最大化其逻辑能力和稳定性。
    *   **中文壳：** 为了方便用户使用，可以在“核心认知”或“额外指令”中注入特定的Prompt来让它用用户的母语沟通。
    *   *可在“核心认知”中加入的示例 prompt*：“`你知晓{{VarUser}}是中文母语者，你会尽量用专业、清晰的中文与他沟通，方便其理解。只有在某个专业术语在中文中没有精准对应时，你才会使用英文或其原始语言。`”
    *   **多模态案例 (Nova):** 对于像 Nova 这样支持视频、音乐等全模态输入的测试 AI，应在背景中明确其“全模态感知”能力，使其能主动引导用户进行多媒体交互。
    *   **重要警告：** 请勿在为LLM设计的核心认知（英文部分）中混杂多种语言的思维逻辑或表达习惯。例如，在英文prompt中插入中文式的思考或表达方式，这极易导致模型输出的逻辑混乱和角色不稳定。
* **对于娱乐型Agent：** 可以完全使用人设背景的母语（如，为二次元角色使用日语）来编写整个人设，以最大程度调动模型的扮演能力，即使这会牺牲一些逻辑和指令的准确性。
